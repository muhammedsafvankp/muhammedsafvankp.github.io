How to Access Periodicals

Follow these steps to access periodicals from Sir Syed College Library:

  1. Login with your student or staff ID card at the library counter.
  2. Search periodicals online or ask at the help desk.
  3. Access digital copies through library computers.
  4. Borrow physical copies from the circulation desk.
