The Economist

Publisher: The Economist Newspaper Limited

Frequency: Weekly

Latest Issue: November 11, 2023

Description

The Economist offers in-depth analysis of current affairs, business, finance, science, technology, and the arts. Known for its global perspective and influential editorial stance.

Access Options

  • In-Library: Find physical copies on Shelf D3 in the Periodicals Section.
  • Digital Access: Available via ProQuest and Flipster (requires library card login).
  • Borrowing: Latest issue is for in-library use only. Back issues can be checked out for 7 days.
